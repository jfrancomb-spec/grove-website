Why Grove Exists

Finding care should never feel uncertain. Inviting someone into your home — whether for your child, a loved one, or daily help — requires real trust. Grove was created to make that trust possible again.

Traditional care platforms focus on listings and volume. Grove focuses on people, accountability, and thoughtful introductions. Instead of endless profiles, we prioritize meaningful local connections.

Grove begins locally in Belle Plaine and Jordan, Minnesota, where reputation still matters and neighbors look out for one another. Our goal is simple: help families feel confident about the people they welcome into their homes.

How Grove Is Different

Grove is built around trust first — not transactions. Every connection starts with clarity, expectations, and community accountability.

  • Carefully Reviewed Caregivers — real people, real accountability.
  • Local Community Focus — trust grows where people know each other.
  • Safety-First Introductions — thoughtful matches instead of endless searching.
