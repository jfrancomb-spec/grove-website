Why Grove Exists

Finding care shouldn’t feel uncertain. Inviting someone into your home — whether for childcare, senior care, pet care, or household help — requires real trust.

Grove was created to bring that trust back by focusing on local relationships and accountability. Instead of endless listings, Grove makes it easier to discover people in your community and connect directly.

Grove begins locally in Belle Plaine and Jordan, Minnesota, where reputation matters and neighbors still look out for one another.
