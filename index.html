Why Grove exists

Finding care shouldn’t feel uncertain. Inviting someone into your home — or walking into someone else’s — requires real trust.

Grove was created to support that trust with stronger profiles, clear expectations, and two-way accountability between families and caregivers.

Instead of endless listings and low-context scrolling, Grove is built around real people, local connections, and better visibility into who you’re trusting.

How Grove works

Create a profile

Families and caregivers build profiles with photos, details, and information that helps the other side feel informed.

Browse with context

Review profiles, trust indicators, care details, reviews, and home information before deciding whether to connect.

Message directly

Once someone looks like a fit, you can start a direct conversation through Grove’s messaging system.

Built for two-way trust

Better visibility

Profiles include photos, care details, experience, and home context to help both sides make more informed decisions.

Two-way reviews

Grove is designed so families and caregivers can both build reputation over time — not just one side.

Direct communication

Messaging makes it easier to ask questions, clarify expectations, and decide whether a connection feels right.

Start with the side that fits you

Grove works best when both families and caregivers can show up with context, clarity, and trust.
