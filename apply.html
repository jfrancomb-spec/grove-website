What families care about most

A short message that feels real and relevant usually works better than a long, generic introduction.

  • Say why this particular job fits your experience or style.
  • Mention the availability that makes you a realistic match.
  • Give one reassuring detail that helps build trust quickly.

A simple message outline

If you are not sure what to write, this structure is usually enough:

  • Open warmly and mention the type of care you noticed.
  • Share the most relevant part of your experience.
  • Mention availability, timing, or location fit.
  • End with a question or invitation to connect.
