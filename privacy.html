Privacy Policy

Grove respects your privacy. Information submitted through this website is used to support connections between families and caregivers, and to operate and improve Grove.

What We Collect

When you submit a care request, apply for a job, or contact a caregiver, we collect the information you enter (such as name, email, phone number, location, schedule details, and your message).

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to display listings, deliver requests/applications to the appropriate party, and maintain safety and quality in the Grove network (for example, reviewing listings before they are visible publicly).

What We Don’t Do

We do not sell personal information.

Data Storage

Submitted details are stored securely and accessed only as needed to operate Grove. We keep data only as long as necessary for operational, safety, and support purposes.

Community Feedback

Grove may support community feedback features over time (such as reviews). If and when these are enabled, we’ll update this policy to explain what is collected and what is shown publicly.

Contact

If you have questions about your data, contact us at hello@trustedgrove.com.
